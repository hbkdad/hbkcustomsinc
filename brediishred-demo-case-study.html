Case Study / Live Website Demo

BREDIISHRED brand shop demo.

This action-sports apparel demo is built around real product photos, a rider-first brand voice, product cards, size selection, a demo cart, and a crew signup flow. It shows HBK can create a live brand experience, not just a basic brochure page.

Open Live Demo See Website Builds

Build Snapshot

  • Use case: Apparel and action-sports brand launch
  • Stack: React, Vite, responsive CSS, Netlify
  • Proof point: Clickable shop-style experience with local brand energy

The Direction

Turn a small apparel drop into a real digital launch hub.

The demo is built to make the first drop feel tangible: visitors can see the products, understand the brand story, click through the gallery, choose sizes, and interact with a cart-style flow before a full Shopify or payment layer is needed.

Deliverables

What HBK built into the demo.

  • Photo-led hero and brand story sections
  • Product cards with prices, tags, and add-to-cart behavior
  • Demo cart with quantity controls and subtotal state
  • Responsive navigation, gallery, size picker, and signup state

Why It Matters

It expands HBK proof beyond trade websites.

01Shows HBK can build branded consumer-facing sites with motion, product flow, and visual personality.
02Gives apparel, merch, and creator businesses a live demo path before investing in a full commerce system.
03Proves the workflow can move from photos and rough direction into a polished, clickable web experience.

Next Step

Use this as proof for brand sites, merch drops, and small ecommerce starts.

For HBK, BREDIISHRED is a strong case study because it proves the same startup-friendly build process can support a visual brand, a launch campaign, and a future shop without pretending the business needs a massive platform on day one.
