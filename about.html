About HBK

A trade-focused startup built to make real businesses look stronger online.

HBK Customs Inc. was built in Timmins with a simple advantage: it comes from the trade world first. The company's job is to help welders, contractors, millwrights, and industrial service businesses present themselves with more clarity, more professionalism, and better lead flow.

Too many strong trade businesses still look smaller, weaker, or less organized online than they actually are in real life. HBK exists to close that gap and build websites, lead systems, and digital presentation that better reflect the business behind the work.
