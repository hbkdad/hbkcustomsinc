Software Concept by HBK

ForgeQuote Shows Where HBK Can Go Beyond Websites.

ForgeQuote is an HBK-built software concept for field quoting, job scoping, PDF estimate generation, and internal workflow support for welders, millwright crews, confined-space teams, mine rescue standby contractors, shutdown crews, and industrial service companies. It exists as a proof-of-capability concept right now, but it points toward a bigger category than brochure sites alone.

Project Snapshot

  • Category: Trade software concept / internal operations app
  • Use case: Field estimates, structured scopes, PDF quotes, and workflow handoff
  • Best-fit audience: Welding, millwright, confined-space, mine rescue standby, industrial service, shutdown, maintenance, and contractor crews
  • Status: Reworked concept demo used as proof of capability for HBK

What It Proves

HBK can build structured internal tools, not just public-facing websites.

  • Trade-specific quoting flow with customer, site, environment, and scope capture
  • Line-item estimating with labour, materials, equipment, travel, and safety categories
  • Offline draft support for field use where signal is unreliable underground or in remote industrial environments
  • PDF output, auth, dashboard logic, and a working app shell instead of a static mockup

Why It Matters

This is a stronger differentiator than another generic agency promise.

  • Most small trade web shops can sell websites, but far fewer can show a believable trade-tech product concept
  • ForgeQuote gives HBK a bridge into custom dashboards, portals, quoting systems, and internal workflow builds
  • It matches the founder story better because it comes from trade-world problems, not generic SaaS ideas
  • It gives industrial and contractor clients a reason to see HBK as a long-term systems partner
Field designed around site visits, notes, measurements, and scope capture
Quote focused on turning trade observations into structured estimates and PDFs
Ops positioned as a foundation for broader workflow and job handoff tools

Where It Could Go

A realistic product path is niche-first, not every trade all at once.

Phase 1

  • Welding, millwright, confined-space, mine rescue standby, and industrial service quoting
  • Templates for shutdown, confined-space, mine rescue, repair, and breakdown work
  • Canadian tax logic, field-ready offline flow, and PDF outputs

Phase 2

  • Approvals, change orders, customer sign-off, and crew handoff
  • Parts, labour-rate libraries, margin tracking, and production feedback
  • Better office-to-field continuity for growing contractors

Phase 3

  • Expansion into adjacent trades once one core wedge is proven
  • More reporting, scheduling, client portals, and repeat-job templates
  • A credible Canadian product brand instead of a thin all-trades promise

HBK Angle

ForgeQuote is best used as a wedge into custom trade software work right now.

For HBK today, ForgeQuote is valuable even before it becomes a standalone product. It tells the market that HBK can build custom quoting systems, operations dashboards, and internal trade tools. That makes the website business more credible immediately, while still leaving room for a larger product play later.
